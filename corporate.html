Design Justifications

Colors

We used 2Gether’s primary palette: Vibrant Blue (#0066FF) for CTAs and highlights, Warm Coral (#FF6B6B) for accents, Soft Yellow (#F8D800) for details, and neutral grays (#333333, #F4F4F4) for balance and contrast. These colors reflect creativity, friendliness, and energy — essential for our creative target audience.

Typography

Montserrat (headings) and Roboto (body text) are both web-safe, clean, and legible fonts. Montserrat gives a bold, modern look while Roboto supports readability at small sizes across devices.

Layout & Navigation

The site uses a consistent nav bar, responsive layout with clear hierarchy, and grid-aligned sections for easy scanning. Each page prioritizes user experience on both mobile and desktop, ensuring accessibility and smooth transitions.

UX Decisions

The interface is designed to inspire creators and remote professionals — with calls to action, open visuals, and intuitive navigation. Each page offers concise but meaningful value, matching our audience’s fast-paced workflow.

Testing Protocol

Participants: 4 freelance creators and remote professionals.

Tasks: Navigate to services, contact the team, understand the brand vision, and identify what the platform offers.

Goals: Test clarity, ease of use, and ability to understand 2Gether’s core offerings.

Findings

  • Navigation was intuitive across all screen sizes
  • Content was clear, but some wanted quicker access to key services
  • Colors and visuals were highly appreciated for energy and vibe

Improvements

  • Add quick-access buttons to core services from the homepage
  • Increase contrast in footer area for readability
  • Consider adding testimonials or user stories
